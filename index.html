Welcome to my planar straight-line graph editor.

Click on empty space to create a point.
Click on a point to highlight it. A highlighted point has a blue rim.
While a point is highlighted, click on another point to connect them with a line segment.
To fix a point in place, highlight it and press "f". To undo, do that again.
Points that are immovable are black.
Press on a line segment to split it.
To delete a line segment or a point, right-click it.

You can drag points around.
You can use the arrow keys to navigate and "+" and "-" to zoom.

After editing, the PSLG will be analyzed.
Points/edges that are part of at least one triangle, non-obtuse angles and non-intersecting edges will be marked green.

To view the unanalyzed PSLG (see fixed/movable edges), hold rightclick on empty space
